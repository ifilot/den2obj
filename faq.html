Creating .d2o files

Why do I see the error “Decompression could not be verified.”?

After compression, Den2Obj checks whether the compression is succesfull by decompressing the stream and tesing for similarity. If the decompressed stream is different from the original stream, this error is thrown automatically. Although it is very rare for this error to be thrown, we have seen it happening for LZMA compression. The recommended work-around is to use a different compression routine, such as gzip or bzip2.
